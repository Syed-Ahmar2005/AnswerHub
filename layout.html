Q: What is responsive design?

A: Responsive design ensures a website looks and works great on phones, tablets, and desktops by adapting layout and typography to the screen size.

Q: How do I center a div?

A: Use a flex container on the parent: display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: center; or use CSS Grid with place-items: center;.
